What is the difference between a merge and a rebase?
"Merging is a safer option that preserves the entire history of your repository, 
while rebasing creates a linear history by moving your feature branch onto the tip of main"
Source: https://www.atlassian.com/git/tutorials/merging-vs-rebasing#:~:text=Merging%20is%20a%20safe%20option,onto%20the%20tip%20of%20main%20.
